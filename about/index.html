Who We Are
Capricorn Healthcare & Special Opportunities (CHSO) is a middle-market private equity fund focused on buyout and growth equity investments in the healthcare and wellness industry. Located in Palo Alto, CA and Washington, DC, the CHSO investment team possesses significant financial and clinical expertise and is supported by a deep relationship network consisting of prominent healthcare executives, clinicians, scientists, regulators, and research institutions
Our Investment Strategy

Partnerships with companies and management teams across numerous healthcare subsectors including services & outsourcing, information technology, healthcare delivery, devices & diagnostics, specialty pharmaceuticals, and wellness

Businesses possessing downside protection and meaningful potential for both revenue and margin growth, rather than utilizing financial engineering, to deliver investment returns

Companies that focus on cost containment, enhanced operating efficiency, and/or improved clinical outcomes

Investment opportunities supporting growth for new products and geographic markets, M&A transactions, and other strategic initiatives

Full and partial sale situations requiring $15MM to $50MM of capital, with the ability to partner with co-investors and other investment firms on larger transactions when appropriate

Thoughtful capital solutions for special situations requiring a flexible and creative financing approach​​
